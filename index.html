Daphné Jouanneteau

My work centers on translating vision into operational reality: setting culture, building teams, designing systems, and holding complexity across brand, people, and execution. I've served as a CEO, an embedded general manager, interim COO and an independent founder, operating across private social clubs, wellness studios, experiential venues, and curated travel.

After a decade of running Lightbox, I've spent recent years embedded across hospitality and lifestyle projects in New York's Hudson Valley, stabilizing early-stage ventures and leading operational buildouts. I'm currently at an inflection point, reassessing where to direct my energy and what structural alignment looks like going forward.

Lightbox

CEO

Lightbox is an experience incubator launching next-generation consumer experiences in purpose-built spaces. I joined as the first hire and later served as CEO, leading the company from its initial build-out through growth, scale, and long-term sustainability. I oversaw all aspects of the business, including business development, operations, finance, and team leadership. During my tenure, I drove consistent year-over-year revenue growth (~30%), secured close to 100 events annually with clients ranging from emerging brands to Fortune 500 companies, and expanded Lightbox's scope across B2B events, community programming, and consumer-facing experiences. I grew the team from zero to fifteen, built operational and financial systems, and worked closely with the board on strategic planning and decision-making. During COVID, I safeguarded the business by securing government funding, retaining employees, and pivoting toward new consumer experience formats in response to a collapsed events market. Lightbox continues to operate today, and I remain involved as an advisor, supporting the team on strategy and key initiatives as needed.

Alchemy

Operations Consultant & Interim General Manager

Alchemy is a yoga and wellness studio offering classes, treatments, and communal spa experiences in Beacon, NY. I led the launch and ongoing operations, overseeing the build-out of systems required to run the business day to day. The role included hiring and managing the full front-of-house team as well as recruiting, onboarding, and contracting teachers and wellness practitioners across disciplines. All scheduling, booking, and customer service workflows were designed and implemented end to end through dedicated software platforms, ensuring a seamless experience for both clients and staff. I helped build Alchemy's operational infrastructure, including staffing models, class and treatment scheduling, payroll coordination, policies, and on-site service standards. In parallel with the team, also shaping the in-space experience, working across layout, flow, lighting, amenities, and guest journey to ensure the studio felt intentional, warm, and well run at every touchpoint. Alchemy operates as both a wellness destination and a community space, balancing operational rigor with a high-touch, human experience.

Les Champs du Sud

Independent Founder

Les Champs du Sud was born from a desire to create a bridge between New York and the French countryside where I grew up, and between modern creativity and deep-rooted rural culture. It's a project rooted in Aveyron's authenticity, its emerging generation of chefs, farmers, winemakers, artisans, and makers who are reclaiming tradition while shaping the region's future. Rather than a conventional tour, I design experiences that feel like invitations into a place: slow mornings exploring landscape and craft, long meals with producers in fields and farms, conversations with winemakers in hidden cellars, and unplanned moments that become the most memorable. Each edition is a curated week that surfaces Aveyron's rhythms and reveals aspects of a region too often overlooked - from its picturesque fields to its still-secret spots and evolving cultural scene. Les Champs du Sud treats travel as immersion: a space for connection, curiosity, and belonging. It is about feeling at home in a landscape that is at once ancient and vibrantly alive.

Decode Experiential

Co-Creator

Decode Experiential was a creative collaboration between Lightbox and As We Are, focused on exploring how digital art can exist meaningfully within experiential and public spaces. Initially launched as a community-driven talk series at Lightbox, Decode combined immersive installations with live conversations featuring digital artists, filmmakers, and technologists. The series gathered hundreds of people from the creative community and was designed to move beyond superficial "experiential" formats, prioritizing genuine interaction with both the work and the artists behind it. The project later scaled into large-format public art with META—morphosis, a month-long digital exhibition at One Times Square, showcasing animated works by 21 digital artists on the building's newly launched screens—temporarily transforming one of the world's most commercialized visual environments into an artist-led, ad-free experience.
